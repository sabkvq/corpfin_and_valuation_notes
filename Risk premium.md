Rate of return in excess of the [[Risk-free rate]]

An [[Expected return]] in excess of that on risk-free securities. The premium provides compensation for the risk of an investment

r_p считается при вычитании из рискового актива [[Risk-free rate]]


$R_i = \alpha_i + \beta_i (R_m) + e_i$
# Market risk premium

$R_m = (r_m - r_f)$
# Index risk premium
$R_i = (r_i - r_f)$

# [[Single-factor model]]
$r − r_f = α + β(r_M − r_f) + e$ 

e - company-specific component
r_m-r_f - market/index risk premium
alpha - stock expected return rate if the market's excess return is zero (r_m - r_f = 0)
